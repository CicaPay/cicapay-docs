Everything you need to accept and send crypto payments, from fully customizable payment flows to ready-to-use hosted interfaces.

CicaPay builds tools that help businesses collect and send crypto payments easily and securely. We handle the complexity of multiple blockchains, multi-currency support, and evolving crypto technologies so you can focus on building your product.

Explore the available options and choose the integration that fits your use case :
