## Pay-in Fees
### Payment Request & Invoice

Pay-in covers both [Payment Requests](/docs/payment-request) and [Invoices](/docs/invoice).
Two parties are charged separately: your customer and you as the merchant.

### Customer Fee

The fee paid by your customer is calculated based on the payment amount and decreases as the amount grows. A minimum fee of **$1.00** applies regardless of the rate.

| Amount Range | Fee Rate | Minimum Fee |
|---|---|---|
| $0 — $100 | **2.5%** | $1.00 |
| $100 — $1,000 | **1.5%** | $1.00 |
| $1,000 — $10,000 | **1.0%** | $1.00 |
| > $10,000 | **0.7%** | $1.00 |

**Example:** A $60 payment → 2.5% = $1.50 fee. A $40 payment → 2.5% = $1.00 (minimum applies).

### Merchant Fee

You are charged a mixed fee combining a percentage and a fixed amount on each pay-in you receive.

| Component | Value |
|---|---|
| Percentage | **2.5%** |
| Fixed amount | **$0.00** |

## Payout Fees

For payouts, only the merchant is charged. Your customer receives the full requested amount.

| Component | Value |
|---|---|
| Percentage | **2%** |
| Fixed amount | — |

<Callout title="Sufficient Balance Required">
The payout fee is deducted on top of the sent amount. To successfully send `$100`, you must have `$100 + fee` available in your wallet. See the [Payout page](/docs/payout) for details.
</Callout>
